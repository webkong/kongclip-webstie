# KongClip

KongClip is a free, fast, private clipboard manager for macOS.

It is built for people who copy and paste constantly and want a calmer workflow: open a compact panel with a shortcut, search clipboard history instantly, pin important items, and paste back into the app you were already using.

## Why KongClip

- Free forever: no subscription, no login, no artificial limits.
- Fast to use: optimized for quick search and quick paste.
- Private by design: clipboard history stays local by default.
- Practical support for real work: text, images, links, and files stay within reach.

## Best for

- Developers managing commands, snippets, and links
- Writers and researchers collecting notes and quotes
- Designers and operators moving text, images, and files between apps
- Anyone on macOS who wants clipboard history without extra noise
